A Recipe For Pizza

Description

Making a pizza is easy!

All you have to do is add sauce, cheese and toppings to your pizza dough.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Stretch the pizza dough out on a baking steel
  2. Spread tomato sauce and mozarella cheese on the pizza dough
  3. Bake on preheated baking steel
  4. Enjoy your delicious meal!
